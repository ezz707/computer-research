Artificial intelligence

Artificial Intelligence is intelligence demonstrated by machines, it's like a simulation to the natural intelligence displayed by humans and animals.
In computer science, artificial intelligence, sometimes called machine intelligence, is any device that perceices its environment and takes actions that maximize its chance of successfully
achieving its goals.

The field of AI research was born at a workshop in 1956, where the term Artificial Intelligence was
coined by John McCarthy to distinguish the field from cybernetics, He and other students produced programs that the press described as "Unbelievable".
Computers were learning checkers game's sstrategies, and it was reported that they played better than the average person.
Further more they were learnt to solve word problems in algebra, proving logical theorems, solving some logical problems.
In late 1990s and till now, AI began to be used for logisctics, data mining, medical diagnosis and other scientific fields.

Nowadays, Artificial Intelligence is known as narrow AI, which is designed to perform a narrow task(Facial recognition or internet searches)
, however, the long-term goal of many reaserchers is to create a strong AI that can outperform humans in general, this might be benefit but also risky.

The ideal characteristic of artificial intelligence is its ability to rationalize and take actions that have the best to achieve a specific goal

When anyone hear the term artificial intelligence, the first thing they usually think of is robots. Which is because films and novels demonstrated a human-like machine that
takes control on earth, which is not true.

AI is based on the principle that human intelligence can be defined in a way that a machine can easily mimix it and execute different tasks, from the simple to the most complex
one. The goals of artificial intelligence include learning, reasoning, perception.
