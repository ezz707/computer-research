How AI can be dangerous

Many researches agree that a superintelligent AI is unlikely to exhibit human emotions like love or hate
and that there's no reason to expect AI to become intentionally benevolent or malevolent.
Instead, when considering how AI might become a risk, experts think two senarios may happen.
The frist senario is that an AI is programmed to be a weapon, in the hands of the wrong person
these weapons could easily cause mass destructions, moreover an AI arms race could lead to an AI war that also results in mass destructions.
The second senario is that AI programmed to do a certain task could develop a destructive method to achieve its goal.
This can happen whenever we fail to fully align the AI's goals with ours, which is stikingly difficult.
These senarios illustrate the concern about about advanced AI, a superintelligent AI will be extremely good at accomplishing its goals, but if this goals aren't aligned with ours we will face a great danger.
